Why we built this

Built by a joinery business owner — and his 16-year-old son.

For the past 6 years, I've been running a joinery business. The biggest problem I kept seeing wasn't the work itself — it was miscommunication between people and departments. Lost orders, forgotten deadlines, and materials running out mid-project.

I realised everything needed to live in one place — simple, clear, and accessible for everyone. That's why Joinery Core was designed around one simple idea: one project = one row across the entire system.

Over time, we added more tools, including stock management and low-stock alerts, because material shortages were constantly affecting production and deadlines.

I designed the workflow based on real workshop experience. My 16-year-old son, who's passionate about programming, helped turn it into a real platform.

Today, we use Joinery Core daily in our own workshop, and the whole team relies on it.

— Piotr, founder · Skylon Joinery
